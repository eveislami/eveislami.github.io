01 About

I work at the seam between AI capability and AI risk. For five years I documented, validated and audited high-risk models — XGBoost, deep neural nets, regression — for global banks, closing 20+ findings against SR 11-7 and the EU AI Act, and managing a 300+ model inventory across three lines of defense.

Now I build GenAI systems myself — grounded LLM apps, validation cockpits, and guardrail layers — to learn their failure modes from the inside. The chapter I'm working toward: validating and auditing GenAI and agentic systems themselves, bringing model-risk rigor to the models I most want to govern.

Off-duty you'll find me chasing the spiciest Chongqing hotpot I can find, hiking, or making digital art.
